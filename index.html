Biography  

Jingyu Gong is an Associate Researcher with the School of Computer Science and Technology, East China Normal University, China. He is a member of Digital Media and Computer Vision Laboratory, working closely with Prof. Lizhuang Ma. He received his B.S. and Ph.D. degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, in 2019 and 2024 respectively. His research interests include computer 3D vision and computer graphics.
